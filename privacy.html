Privacy Policy

Maine Winters LLC and all of its products are built on a simple principle: we collect only what we need and never sell your data.

Last updated: April 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to all products and services operated by Maine Winters LLC, including:

QuickClips QuickPics ClipHook SponsorKit QuickFocus InspectCSS PageSnap DarkSight TextTone SummarizeAI TabFlow MailPing PriceWatch LinkedOut ClearCart QuickReview ReplyAI SnapRecord GitHelper FocusTime QuickApply ReadMode HoverWord PageMark

By using any Maine Winters LLC product, you agree to this policy. If you have questions, email us at hello@mainewinters.com.

1. Who We Are

Maine Winters LLC is a software company based in Maine, USA. We build AI-powered tools for content creators, families, and productivity-focused individuals. We are a single-member LLC with no investors and no advertising partners.

Contact: hello@mainewinters.com
Website: mainewinters.com

2. What Data We Collect

Account data (SaaS products: QuickClips, QuickPics, SponsorKit, ClipHook)

  • Name and email address (provided at signup via Clerk authentication)
  • Google or other OAuth provider ID (if you sign in with Google)
  • Subscription plan and billing status (managed by Stripe — we do not store card numbers)

Content you upload

  • QuickClips: Videos you upload are stored in Cloudflare R2 and processed to generate clips. Your videos are never shared with other users or used to train AI models.
  • QuickPics: Photos you upload are stored in Cloudflare R2 to generate your slideshow. Your photos are never shared or used to train AI models.
  • SponsorKit: Sponsorship deal information you enter is stored in our database and is private to your account.

Usage data

  • Number of videos/photos processed, clips generated, and API calls made (for billing and plan enforcement)
  • Basic request logs (IP address, timestamp, endpoint) retained for up to 30 days for security and debugging

Payment data

All payment processing is handled by Stripe. We store only your Stripe customer ID and subscription status. We never see or store your credit card number, CVV, or billing address.

3. Chrome Extensions — Specific Data Practices

Each extension has its own data scope. Here is what each one accesses:

QuickFocus

Accesses your approximate location (city-level) via a public weather API to show local weather on your new tab page. No location data is stored or transmitted to Maine Winters LLC servers. No account required.

InspectCSS

Reads the CSS of webpages you visit to display inspection results. All processing occurs locally in your browser. No page content or CSS data is transmitted to any server. No account required.

PageSnap

Captures screenshots of pages when you explicitly trigger a capture. Screenshots are stored locally in your browser's storage. No screenshots are uploaded or transmitted without your explicit action. No account required.

DarkSight

Reads and modifies page CSS to apply dark mode styling. All processing is local. No browsing data is collected or transmitted. Per-site preferences are stored locally in your browser. No account required.

TextTone

When you select text and choose a rewrite tone, the selected text is sent to the AI provider you configure (OpenAI or Anthropic) using your own API key. Maine Winters LLC does not receive, store, or log any text you rewrite. Your API key is stored locally in your browser's extension storage and is never sent to Maine Winters LLC servers.

SummarizeAI

When you trigger a summary, the text content of the active webpage is sent to the AI provider you configure (OpenAI or Anthropic) using your own API key. Maine Winters LLC does not receive, store, or log any page content you summarize. Summaries are displayed in the side panel and not stored persistently.

TabFlow

Accesses your open browser tabs to display, search, and organize them. Tab data (titles and URLs) is stored locally in your browser's extension storage for session management. No tab data is transmitted to any server. No account required.

MailPing

When you enable tracking on a Gmail message, a tracking pixel hosted on our server (mailping.mainewinters.com) is embedded in the email. When the recipient opens the email and images load, our server logs the open event (timestamp, approximate location, device type) and sends you a browser notification. We store open event data only for emails you choose to track and only accessible to you. We do not track emails sent to you.

PriceWatch

Reads product page content on supported shopping sites (Amazon, Walmart, Target, Best Buy, eBay) to extract and monitor prices. Price data for products you track is stored locally in your browser. Price checks are performed by your browser directly against the retailer's website — not proxied through our servers. No shopping behavior data is collected or sold. No account required.

LinkedOut

Reads LinkedIn profile and search result pages that you are actively viewing in your browser. Profile data is converted to CSV entirely within your browser. No data is transmitted to any server. No LinkedIn credentials or session tokens are accessed or stored. No account with Maine Winters LLC is required.

ClipHook

When you click the ClipHook extension on a YouTube page, the video's transcript is sent to our ClipHook API server to generate hook suggestions using the Anthropic API. We do not store the transcript or the generated hooks after the response is delivered. We log only request metadata (timestamp, YouTube video ID) for rate limiting purposes, retained for 7 days.

ClearCart

Reads product page content and checkout page content on supported shopping sites (Amazon, Walmart, Target, Best Buy, eBay, Etsy, Home Depot, Lowes, Kohls, Macys) to display coupon codes and track prices. All coupon codes are stored locally in the extension. Price tracking data (product title, URL, price history, and your target alert price) is stored entirely in your browser's local extension storage. No shopping behavior, browsing history, or personal data is transmitted to Maine Winters LLC servers. No account required.

QuickReview

When you click "Summarize Reviews" on a product page, the review text visible on that page is sent directly to OpenAI using your own API key. Maine Winters LLC does not receive, store, or log any review text or product information. Your OpenAI API key is stored locally in your browser's extension storage and is never transmitted to Maine Winters LLC servers. Usage counts (summaries per month) are stored locally only. No account required.

ReplyAI

When you generate a reply, the email body text visible in your Gmail or Outlook window is sent directly to OpenAI using your own API key. Maine Winters LLC does not receive, store, or log any email content you process. Your OpenAI API key is stored locally in your browser's extension storage and is never transmitted to Maine Winters LLC servers. No account required.

SnapRecord

Records your screen or active browser tab using the browser's built-in MediaRecorder and tab capture APIs. All recorded video is saved directly to your local Downloads folder as a WebM file. No video data is uploaded to or transmitted through Maine Winters LLC servers. Recording history metadata (filename, duration, file size) is stored locally in browser extension storage. No account required.

GitHelper

When you select code and choose an analysis mode, the selected code is sent directly to OpenAI using your own API key. Maine Winters LLC does not receive, store, or log any code you analyze. Your OpenAI API key is stored locally in your browser's extension storage and is never transmitted to Maine Winters LLC servers. Works on GitHub, GitLab, and Bitbucket pages. No account required.

FocusTime

Manages Pomodoro focus timers and blocks distracting websites during active focus sessions using Chrome's declarativeNetRequest API. Your blocklist, session preferences, and focus statistics are stored entirely in your browser's local extension storage. No browsing data or site-blocking activity is transmitted to any server. No account required.

QuickApply

Stores your job application profile (name, email, phone, address, work history, and other fields) in your browser's local extension storage and uses it to auto-fill job application forms on supported sites. Your profile data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to Maine Winters LLC servers or any third party. No account required.

ReadMode

Reads the content of webpages you are actively viewing to extract and display articles in a clean reader format. All processing occurs locally in your browser. Any highlights you save are stored in your browser's local extension storage. No page content, highlights, or reading history is transmitted to any server. No account required.

HoverWord

When you double-click a word, the word is sent to the free Dictionary API (dictionaryapi.dev) to retrieve its definition — no account or API key required for definitions. When you select a phrase and request a translation, the selected text is sent directly to OpenAI using your own API key. Maine Winters LLC does not receive, store, or log any words or phrases you look up or translate. Your OpenAI API key is stored locally in your browser's extension storage. No account with Maine Winters LLC required.

PageMark

Reads the title, meta description, and open graph data of webpages you choose to save. All bookmark data (URL, title, description, auto-generated tags, and personal notes) is stored entirely in your browser's local extension storage. No bookmark data is transmitted to any server. No account required. Free plan stores up to 100 pages locally.

6. Data Retention

  • Uploaded videos and photos: Retained while your account is active. Deleted within 30 days of account deletion.
  • Account data: Retained while your account is active. Deleted within 30 days of account deletion upon request.
  • Request logs: Retained for 30 days, then automatically deleted.
  • MailPing open events: Retained for 90 days, then automatically deleted.
  • Stripe billing records: Retained as required by law (typically 7 years).
